Fun Stuff

Hobby 1: Yoyo

I have played with the yoyo on and off for almost 10 years now. I first started when I was around 11 years old, and tried to learn as many tricks as I could.

Now, I find new tricks to be exceedingly difficult to learn, but I still practice occasionally so I don't forget the tricks I have learned. I also enjoy watching the World Yoyo Contest to see the crazy tricks some people can do that I could never even attempt.

Our Family Pet Snake

Allow me to introduce our pet snake, affectionately referred to as "the Snake", or "Snaky".

He (at least I think it's a he, nobody knows for sure) enjoys eating mice and taking the occasional soak in his tub. While he may look threatening at times, especially since he is now almost 6 ft long, he is in fact a docile, nonvenomous Bullsnake.

One of the nice things about our snake is that he requires very little care. All he needs is to be feed and cleaned once a week. In addition, over the winter, the snake enters a state of semi-hibernation, where he lowers his body temperature and doesn't eat or do anything for more than 3 months.
